Purpose of the Website

The purpose of this website is to promote local tourism by showcasing famous tourist destinations, cultural heritage, and traditional food. It is designed as a static website using basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Why Local Tourism?

  • Supports local economy
  • Preserves cultural traditions
  • Affordable travel option
  • Environment friendly
