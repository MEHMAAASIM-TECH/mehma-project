Local culture reflects traditions, customs, and lifestyle. Traditional food is an important part of cultural identity. Tourists enjoy tasting local dishes and learning about food history.

Traditional meals

Traditional meals are prepared using local spices and recipes passed down through generations.

Street Food

Street food is popular among tourists for its taste, affordability, and cultural experience.

Traditional Sweets

Local sweets are made on festivals and special occasions and represent cultural celebrations.
